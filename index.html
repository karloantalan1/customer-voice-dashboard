Revenue estimates are based on GPV figures explicitly mentioned in Slack posts and Dixa VOC summaries. Where no GPV was stated, estimates use segment median GPV ($8K/mo solo, $45K/mo small team, $120K/mo enterprise).

Known Limitations

Slack-only scope. This excludes Dixa raw tickets (~15K/mo), NPS verbatims, Typeform feature requests, Facebook community (~50K members), App Store reviews, and Gong call transcripts. Slack over-indexes on AE/CX-reported feedback, which skews toward larger accounts and louder voices.

Selection bias. What gets posted to Slack ≠ what matters most. High-frequency small-account issues may be underrepresented. Cross-reference with Dixa ticket volume data (Hex dashboard) before making roadmap decisions.

Revenue estimates are directional, not precise. Use for relative prioritization, not financial modeling.
